Move the World

Convey your message to a global audience.

Enjoy quality content covering a diverse range of topics from other authors around the globe.

Value Quality, not Quantity

Authors are charged based on the size of content they contribute, which incentivices brevity while letting people 'put their money where their mouth is'.

Authors are rewarded when other users reply to thier content; higher quality, more replies, deeper discussions, more rewards.

Feed Fairies, not Trolls

By only repling to messages of value to the community, quality content is incentivized.

If a troll does reply to your content, ignore them while enjoying the tokens you've earned from them!

Immutable, Indelible, and Indefinite

Messaged conveyed over this platform are mined into a blockchain and distributed across the network to ensure they cannot be censored, modified, or deleted.
