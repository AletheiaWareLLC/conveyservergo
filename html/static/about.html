This digital age bombards society with a firehose of information. Convey aims to increase the signal to noise ratio by incentivizing quality over quantity. Authors pay to contribute to the onslaught of information vying for our finite attention, literally putting their money where their mouth is, and in return are rewarded by the discussion generated in the replies from the community.

Quality over Quantity

Authors are charged per Byte they contribute, which incentivices brevity and let's people 'put their money where their mouth is'. Authors are rewarded when other users reply to thier content; the higher quality, the more replies, the deeper the discussion, the more reward.

Feed Fairies, not Trolls

By only repling to Messages of value to the Community, quality content is incentivized. If a Troll replies to your Content, ignore them but enjoy the Tokens you've earned from them!
